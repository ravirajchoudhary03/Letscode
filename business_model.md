# Business Model & Monetization Strategy

## Core Model: B2B SaaS Subscription
MarketEcho generates recurring revenue through tiered licensing, targeting D2C brands, marketing agencies, and product teams.

### 1. Starter Tier (Free or Low Cost)
*   **Target:** Early-stage startups, solo founders.
*   **Pricing:** Free or $19—$49/mo.
*   **Features:**
    *   Track 1 Brand.
    *   Basic Mention Counts (Reddit & YouTube).
    *   7-History Window.
    *   *Purpose: User acquisition and product validation.*

### 2. Growth Tier (Core Revenue)
*   **Target:** Growing D2C brands, Marketing Agencies.
*   **Pricing:** $199—$499/mo.
*   **Features:**
    *   Track up to 5 Competitors per dashboard.
    *   **Advanced Analytics:** Share of Voice (SOV), Sentiment Analysis, Market Index Score.
    *   **Alerts:** Real-time spikes in negative/positive sentiment.
    *   **Reporting:** PDF/CSV exports for stakeholder meetings.
    *   *Purpose: Validating ROI for marketing teams.*

### 3. Enterprise Tier
*   **Target:** Large Corporations, Conglomerates.
*   **Pricing:** Custom ($2k+/mo).
*   **Features:**
    *   Unlimited Brand Tracking.
    *   **API Access:** Integrate MarketEcho data into internal BI tools (Tableau, PowerBI).
    *   **Custom Sources:** Add specific forums or niche sites.
    *   Dedicated Account Manager & SLA.

## Future Upsells (Expansion Revenue)
1.  **AI Recommendations:** "Generated Action Plans" based on sentiment (e.g., "Draft a response to this Reddit thread").
2.  **Influencer Discovery:** Identify top voices driving the conversation for a brand.
